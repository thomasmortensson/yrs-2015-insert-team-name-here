Spoiler Alert was made for people who like to watch a programme without any spoilers from people. This application will get rid of all the spoilers that will spoil all the shows you are watching.

All you have to do to download this useful app is:

1. Download it from our website.

2. Select the program you are watching.

3. Hit the 'Done' button.

Spoiler Alert will not affect any other apps on your device it will only hide spoiler posts.
