Description

Spoiler Alert is an extension for people who like to watch a programme without getting any spoilers from people on social media/s.

This application will get rid of all the posts about the show you have seleted on the social media/s you have selected.

All you have to do is:

1. Download it from our website.

2. Select a show

3. Select a social network

4.Hit the 'Done' button.

Spoiler Alert will not affect any other apps on your device it will only hide spoiler posts.
